About STEM by Step

Why STEM by Step? STEM by Step was created to simplify the research journey for students taking on their first project. It fills the gap left by jargon-heavy guides and outdated methods.

Who it's for: High school and early college students with a passion for research but no idea where to start.

What makes it different: Clear, concise, modern, and friendly. No fluff, just what you need—step by step.

Vision: Empower youth with tools to not only learn science but contribute to it meaningfully.
