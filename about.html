About SBS

STEM by Step (SBS) breaks down the complexities of research into clear, actionable steps. We believe that groundbreaking research shouldn't be gatekept by complexity – it should be accessible to every aspiring innovator who has the drive to make an impact.

Our platform simplifies the research journey through practical, step-by-step guidance. From ideation to execution, we provide carefully curated resources and modern tools that demystify the research process, making it more approachable for students and early-career researchers.

Our Founder

STEM by Step was founded by Genavive Ramirez, a high school senior passionate about bioengineering and data science. Through her own research experiences, she recognized the need for a more accessible approach to conducting high-impact research.

Driven by her commitment to democratizing research opportunities, Genavive created SBS to provide young innovators with a clear roadmap for their research journey. Her vision is to empower the next generation of researchers with the tools and confidence they need to pursue their scientific aspirations.
